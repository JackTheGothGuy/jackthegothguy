Midnight Glitter

Stars whisper secrets in a violet sky,

Soft shadows dance where the moonflowers lie.

The night hums a song of a world made new,

And every bright dream is waiting for you.

★ Poem Placeholder #2 ★

Paper Wings

A letter of longing folds into flight,

Carried by hope through the hush of the night.

Words softly flutter on paper-thin wings,

Sharing the wonder that loving life brings.

★ Poem Placeholder #3 ★

Summer Echo

Warm wind carries laughter down the street,

Bright days in the sun feel so sweet.

Remember this moment when shadows grow long,

And hold it forever inside your own song.
